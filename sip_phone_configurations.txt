Linksys IP Phone SPA942
-----------------------
* IP Address
  press settings key followed by 9

* Basic configuration:
- Click "Admin Login" on upper right corner
- Click Ext 1
- In Proxy and Registration section configure: Proxy and Register
- In Subscriber Information: Enter Display Name, Password, User ID


* Enable reliable provisional responses
Advanced->Ext 1->SIP Settings->SIP 100REL Enable.

* Username and Credentials
Admin Login-> Ext 1 -> Subscriber Information

* Notes:
icmp from mand (host_debug) causes the phone to show unregistered status.
